About

Graham Johnson. BA fr Columbia University in American Studies. Working at Consortia and Are.na. Manet > Monet; SMiLE > Sgt. Pepper's.

Interests: Post-rationalism (?), metacriticism, Trilling, Schmidhuber, 4chan, religion, pragmatism, Russian formalism, counter-signaling, sustainability, metamod, nostalgia, Weird Suns, RCs, NBA, Random Cloud.

Recent projects:

  • Working towards a theory of literary compression (in progress).
    Update: Preliminary sketch of theory here.

  • Working towards a predictive processing model of aesthetics (research channel)
  • Developing the concept of generic fitness, or generic "fit," as laid out in a post for Suspended Reason.

  • Developing a concept of critical proxies as initially sketched out in the final section of this essay.
    Update: Concept refurbished for "Intro to Cargocult," 11/24/16.

  • Managing editor for the first U.S. undergraduate journal of art criticism (JAC).

Managing editor for Rare Candy, a print and online music magazine dedicated to fostering underground music.

Some books I read this yr.

Posted by Reynard Seifert @ 10:05 pm on December 28th, 2013

The other day I was eating from a large tin of popcorn. Someone asked which is your favorite. Thru chews I said I like them all / for different reasons. That’s how I feel about these books.

“the law of genre”


Red Doc>
Anne Carson

Our Gertrude Stein wears prescription sunglasses.

 The Science Delusion
Curtis White

If you like pop science, this is going to hurt a little.

When Species Meet
Donna Haraway

Remember the word natureculture. It will be a key in the next level.

The Affect Theory Reader
Melissa Gregg, Gregory J. Seigworth

The road ahead is dark & broad, better put your feelings on.

Joseph Walser’s Machine
Gonçalo M. Tavares

Very like a crystal — shiny & sad, trapped in itself.

Art on the Edge
Harold Rosenberg

At a specific time, this book served a specific purpose, and remains as relevant as ever.

Codex Seraphinanus
Luigi Serafini

One loop around the tree is I.

Moby-Dick
Herman Melville

Written long before the others, eerily, it contains them.

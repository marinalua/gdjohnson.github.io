Posted by Brooks Sterritt @ 12:09 am on December 11th, 2012

New York Tyrant/Tyrant Books has recently brought two more important texts into the world.

STRANGE COWBOY BY SAM MICHEL

SKY SAW BY BLAKE BUTLER

More heroic motivators: the comment thread!
You feel a desire to break the pattern. There have been threats throughout history, and if nothing changes, threats will keep winning evermore. This framing is related to 1: it’s a sense that the existent universal rules support the threat, and you intend to break those rules.
You feel a desire to see what the future holds for your [family/city/universe], and want to eliminate the threat to that end. This is closely related to 5.
You feel that those put in danger by the threat are your allies, teammates, siblings. They’re awesome, and they would protect you, which drives your urge to stand by them. This is related to 4.
